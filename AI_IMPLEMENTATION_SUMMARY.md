## 🔬 How AI Works (Simple Explanation)

### **Data Collection:**
AI looks at your last 30 days of sales:
- What sold?
- How much?
- When?
- For how much?

### **Analysis:**
AI calculates:
- Average daily sales per product
- Growth trends (up/down/stable)
- Patterns (busy days, slow days)
- Stock consumption rate

### **Prediction:**
AI forecasts:
- How much you'll sell next 7 days
- When you'll run out
- What to order and how much

### **Confidence:**
AI rates reliability:
- 80-100%: Trust it!
- 50-79%: Good guide
- 0-49%: Be cautious (new product/erratic sales)

### **Recommendation:**
AI suggests:
- Order quantities (2 weeks supply)
- Urgency level (critical/high/medium/low)
- Cost estimates
- Actions to take
